Flexible

Chainer supports sophisticated neural network architectures including feed-forward nets, convnets, recurrent nets and recursive nets. It further accepts per-batch architectures.

Intuitive

Chainer is based on dynamic network definition. Forward computation can include any control flow statements of Python without lacking ability of backpropagation. It makes codes intuitive and easy to debug.

Powerful

Chainer supports CUDA computation. It only requires few lines of codes to leverage a GPU. It also runs on multiple GPUs with a little effort.
